About Dragon Ball Anime

Dragon Ball is a Japanese anime and manga series created by Akira Toriyama. It was first serialized in Weekly Sh≈çnen Jump magazine in 1984, and it quickly became one of the most popular and influential anime series worldwide.


The story follows the adventures of Goku, a Saiyan warrior, as he explores the world, meets new friends, and battles powerful foes. Along the way, he collects the mystical Dragon Balls, which, when gathered together, summon a wish-granting dragon.


Throughout its long run, Dragon Ball has spawned multiple anime series, movies, video games, and merchandise, making it a cultural phenomenon that has captivated audiences of all ages.
