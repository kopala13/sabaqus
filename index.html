Dragon Ball began as a manga series written and illustrated by Akira Toriyama and serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump from 1984 to 1995, after concluding his previous mega-hit manga Doctor Slump, and spawned a franchise that would become a global phenomenon. Dragon Ball chronicles the adventures of a cheerful monkey boy named Son Goku, in a story that was initially inspired by the Chinese tale Journey to the West

In the first third of the Dragon Ball manga (which served as the source material for the eponymous anime), Goku and a huge cast of friends and enemies search for the magical Dragon Balls that they could use to make a wish come true, and encounter many characters and adventures along the way.
